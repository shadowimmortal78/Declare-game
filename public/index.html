Quick guide

How to play

01

Play

Lay a single, a same-rank group, or a suited sequence of 3-4 cards.

02

Pick up

Draw one card from the deck or choose one card from the previous play.

03

Connect

Match linked ranks between plays to skip your pickup entirely.

04

Declare

After one full turn cycle, declare with 15 points or fewer. A lower opposing hand defeats you.

Lowest cumulative score survives. Reach the point limit and you are eliminated after your optional re-entry.
